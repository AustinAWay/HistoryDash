WHAP exam format?	55 MCQs (40%) + 3 SAQs (20%) + 1 DBQ (25%) + 1 LEQ (15%)	WHAP::Exam
What is a SAQ?	Short Answer Question - answer parts a/b/c directly, no thesis needed	WHAP::Exam
What is a DBQ?	Document-Based Question - analyze 7 documents, write thesis-driven essay with outside evidence	WHAP::Exam
What is a LEQ?	Long Essay Question - no documents, use your knowledge, thesis-driven argument	WHAP::Exam
How many units in WHAP?	9 units (covers 1200 CE to present)	WHAP::Exam

APGOV exam format?	55 MCQs (80 min, 50%) + 4 FRQs (100 min, 50%)	APGOV::Exam
APGOV FRQ types (all 4)?	1) Concept Application 2) Quantitative Analysis 3) SCOTUS Comparison 4) Argument Essay	APGOV::Exam
What is APGOV FRQ 1 (Concept Application)?	Apply a political concept to a real-world scenario	APGOV::Exam
What is APGOV FRQ 2 (Quantitative Analysis)?	Interpret data/chart and explain political implications	APGOV::Exam
What is APGOV FRQ 3 (SCOTUS Comparison)?	Compare a required case to a non-required case on similar principles	APGOV::Exam
What is APGOV FRQ 4 (Argument Essay)?	Develop an argument using evidence (like a mini-LEQ)	APGOV::Exam
How many units in APGOV?	5 units	APGOV::Exam
